BLAZE EXCHANGE × ARTIST COLLABORATION AGREEMENT

Artist Brand Ambassador Collaboration Agreement

This Artist Brand Ambassador Collaboration Agreement (“Agreement”) is made and entered into on 3rd April, 2026, by and between:

1. Parties

Brand: Blaze Exchange

Artist / Influencer:

Full Legal Name: Muje Williams
Stage Name: Muje Spark

Together referred to as “the Parties.”

2. Purpose of Agreement

The purpose of this Agreement is to establish a 30-day promotional collaboration campaign in which the Artist will act as a Brand Ambassador for Blaze Exchange by creating promotional content, driving audience participation through referral code MUJE0321, and supporting the Fan Wall Spotlight Campaign.

3. Campaign Term

This Agreement shall commence on 7th April, 2026 and shall end on 7th May, 2026, unless terminated earlier in accordance with this Agreement.

Campaign duration: 30 Days

4. Scope of Work / Deliverables

The Artist agrees to deliver the following during the campaign period:

Weekly Deliverables

  • 2 TikTok videos
  • 2 Instagram reels
  • 3–5 Instagram story slides
  • 1 livestream session weekly
  • 1 X/Twitter promotional post
  • Daily WhatsApp status reminders
  • 1 Fan Wall Spotlight repost weekly

Monthly Deliverables

  • 8 TikTok videos
  • 8 Instagram reels
  • 20+ Instagram story slides
  • 4 livestream sessions
  • 4 X/Twitter posts
  • 4 Fan Wall Spotlight reposts

All content must naturally promote:

  • Blaze Exchange services
  • Referral code MUJE0321
  • Fan Wall Spotlight participation
  • Livestream collaborations where applicable

5. Referral Code & Tracking

The Artist shall actively promote the dedicated referral code: MUJE0321.

Blaze Exchange shall provide:

  • A unique referral link
  • Automatic referral tracking system
  • Signup and completed trade reports
  • Fan Wall qualification data
  • Leaderboard records

All signups and completed trades generated through the referral link or code shall be automatically attributed to the Artist.

6. Fan Wall Spotlight Campaign

The major incentive for the Artist’s audience shall be the Official Fan Wall Spotlight.

Users who trade using MUJE0321 may qualify for:

  • Weekly spotlight features
  • Reposts by the Artist
  • Reposts by Blaze Exchange
  • Hall of Fame recognition
  • Livestream shoutouts
  • Top Supporter Wall placement

Blaze Exchange shall manage:

  • Leaderboard integrity
  • Qualification criteria
  • Weekly spotlight selection
  • Final Hall of Fame recognition

7. Compensation

The compensation for this collaboration shall be as mutually discussed and agreed by both parties prior to campaign commencement.

Any performance-based incentives, bonuses, or private benefits agreed outside this document shall remain valid between both parties.

8. Content Approval & Compliance

The Artist agrees that all campaign content must align with Blaze Exchange’s approved messaging and brand tone.

Blaze Exchange reserves the right to request:

  • Revisions
  • Caption edits
  • Compliance corrections
  • Removal of misleading financial claims
  • Removal of non-approved promotional angles

Sponsored content must be disclosed clearly where required.

9. Exclusivity

During the campaign term, the Artist shall not publicly promote any competing digital asset exchange platform without prior written consent from Blaze Exchange.

This exclusivity applies only between: 7th April, 2026 – 7th May, 2026.

10. Intellectual Property & Usage Rights

All campaign content created by the Artist under this Agreement may be:

  • Reposted by Blaze Exchange
  • Used in paid advertisements
  • Used as website testimonials/social proof
  • Archived for future brand campaigns

Usage license: 12 months from campaign completion date.

The Artist retains ownership of original creative content, while Blaze Exchange receives a commercial usage license.

11. Confidentiality

The Artist agrees to keep confidential all non-public information shared during the campaign, including:

  • Internal offers
  • Campaign analytics
  • Performance reports
  • Referral tracking insights
  • Compensation discussions
  • Internal conversion strategy

This clause survives for: 12 months after campaign completion.

12. Termination

Either party may terminate this Agreement if:

  • Deliverables are repeatedly missed
  • Agreed obligations are not met
  • Misconduct damages brand reputation
  • Fake traffic or fraudulent metrics are discovered
  • Confidentiality is breached
  • The Artist promotes a direct competitor during the term

Where termination occurs, obligations shall be prorated to completed deliverables where applicable.

13. Governing Law

This Agreement shall be governed by and interpreted in accordance with the laws of the Federal Republic of Nigeria.
